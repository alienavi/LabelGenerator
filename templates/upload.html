Label layout

Upload a workbook that includes the columns Name, Carry Out, and Dine in. Carry-out orders are rendered as 3 columns × 10 rows per page. Each person receives one label showing the total carry-out count; if their total exceeds two, extra labels containing only the name are added to match the packing algorithm (⌊count ÷ 2⌋ + remainder).

Labels are sorted alphabetically by name. The final page lists dine-in totals in a formatted table.

Tips:

  • Enter numbers only in the Carry Out and Dine in columns; blanks are treated as zero.
  • Keep names consistent to ensure totals aggregate correctly.
